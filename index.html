Heard internship

Recently I’ve been doing an internship at Heard and here is a review of what I’ve accomplished.

In short, Heard is a new social network where users get personalized content based on their interests and behavior without the need to follow or friend anyone.

As an intern, I’m primarily building and testing the back-end scalable user clustering and post recommendation system, based on a multi-queue structure, using Java, MongoDB and Azure. I’m also partly responsible for maintaining REST API, user activity tracking system and post inboxing system.
